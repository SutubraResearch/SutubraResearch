Long Duration Energy Storage

and its outlook in Nova Scotia

The research project, funded through Nova Scotia's Net Zero Emerging Concepts and Technologies Research Program, seeks to determine the potential role of long-duration energy storage in achieving a decarbonized electricity system in Nova Scotia by 2050. This work leverages and will enhance the open-source Atlantic Canada Energy System model, which was previously developed by Sutubra Research, and will be done in partnership with Dr. Lukas Swan of the Renewable Energy Storage Laboratory at Dalhousie University.

Temoa and the Open Energy Outlook

I'm working with teams at Carnegie Mellon University and NC State University to advance the popular open-source energy system model Temoa and the Open Energy Outlook. The OEO, which uses Temoa as the underlying modelling engine, "aims examines U.S. energy futures to inform energy and climate policy efforts by applying the gold standards of policy-focused academic modeling, maximizing transparency, and building a networked community."
